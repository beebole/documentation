<Update label="March 2026" tags={["Integrations", "Approvals", "Reports", "Notifications", "Authentication"]}>
  You can now connect Beebole to Linear to import your projects, issues, and team members for time tracking. Your Linear workspace stays in sync automatically with Beebole.

  The Asana integration has been revamped with a new configuration flow, including workspace selection and the choice to import tasks for planning or project time tracking.

  You can now connect Beebole to Jira to synchronize your Jira projects and issues for time tracking and resource planning in Beebole.

  Beebole now supports approval workflows. Submit timesheets for review with a dedicated approval pane, automated reminders for pending approvals, and configurable routing.

  Tags can now be linked as relational data, with date overlap checks and schedule support for more flexible project and resource organization.

  The notification system has been revamped with email templates, absence quota alerts, per-person and per-organization settings, and real-time websocket notifications.

  Custom fields are now available in reports, with support for recursive team and project hierarchies.

  Archived team members can now be restored directly from the archive.

  Sign-in with passkeys is now smoother, with an improved WebAuthn authentication flow.

  Navigation has been improved: click to view details, meta-click to open in a new tab, and shift-click to filter.
</Update>
