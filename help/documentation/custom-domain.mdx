Beebole's custom domain feature lets you replace the default Beebole URL with your own branded address -- for example, `time.yourcompany.com`. Your team signs in at a URL that matches your company identity, while everything else works exactly the same.

<Info>
  Custom domains are available on select plans. Check your [subscription](/documentation/subscription) to confirm availability.
</Info>

---

## How it works

When you configure a custom domain, your team accesses Beebole through your chosen URL instead of the standard `app.beebole.com` address. This involves:

1. Choosing a subdomain (e.g., `time.yourcompany.com`)
2. Adding a DNS record that points your subdomain to Beebole
3. Activating the custom domain in your Beebole account settings

Beebole handles the SSL certificate automatically, so your custom domain is always served over HTTPS.

---

## Setting up a custom domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your subdomain">
    Decide on the subdomain you want to use. A common pattern is `time.yourcompany.com` or `beebole.yourcompany.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account** and find the **Custom domain** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your domain">
    Type your chosen subdomain in the **Custom domain** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your DNS">
    Log in to your domain registrar or DNS provider and create a **CNAME** record pointing your subdomain to the value shown in Beebole. The exact target is displayed on the settings page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify and activate">
    Return to Beebole and click **Verify**. Once the DNS record is detected, your custom domain becomes active. DNS propagation can take up to 48 hours, though it typically completes within minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You need access to your company's DNS settings to complete this setup. If you are unsure, ask your IT team to create the CNAME record for you.
</Note>

---

## Removing a custom domain

To remove your custom domain and revert to the default Beebole URL:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account** and find the **Custom domain** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the domain">
    Clear the custom domain field and click **Save**. Your team will need to use the standard Beebole URL going forward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clean up DNS">
    Optionally, remove the CNAME record from your DNS provider to keep your DNS configuration tidy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a root domain instead of a subdomain?">
    Beebole requires a subdomain (e.g., `time.yourcompany.com`). Root domains (e.g., `yourcompany.com`) are not supported because CNAME records cannot be set on root domains with most DNS providers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the custom domain served over HTTPS?">
    Yes. Beebole automatically provisions and renews an SSL certificate for your custom domain. All traffic is encrypted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does DNS propagation take?">
    DNS changes typically propagate within a few minutes, but it can take up to 48 hours in some cases. If verification fails, wait and try again later.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will existing bookmarks and links to app.beebole.com still work?">
    Yes. The standard Beebole URL continues to work even after you set up a custom domain. Your team can use either address to sign in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
