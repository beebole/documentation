In Beebole, **People** represents every user or team member in your account — anyone who tracks time, manages projects, or views reports. Each person has a profile that combines their user account with configuration data like rates, schedules, and permissions.

<Info>
  Every person is assigned a role that determines what they can see and do. Beebole includes default roles (Administrator, Manager, Employee), and you can create custom roles to match your organization's structure. See [Roles & permissions](/help/documentation/roles-authorisations).
</Info>

---

## Adding your team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the People page">
    Click **People** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a person">
    Click the **[+]** icon. Enter the person's **Name**, **Email**, and **Role**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bulk import (optional)">
    To add a large team at once, use the **Import** option. You can paste names and emails directly from a spreadsheet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The person's profile is now created in your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Adding a person and inviting them are two separate actions. A person's profile exists as soon as you save it, but they cannot log in until you send an invitation.
</Note>

---

## Sending invitations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the person's profile">
    Click the person's name in the People list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send Invitation**. Beebole emails a secure link for them to set their password and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track status">
    The profile shows a **Pending** status until the person completes their sign-up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can resend invitations at any time from the person's profile if they missed the initial email.
</Tip>

## Configuring person profiles

Click a person's name to access their profile settings:

- **Manager** — Assign who approves this person's timesheets.
- **Tags** — Organize people into departments, teams, or locations. See [Tags](/help/documentation/tags).
- **Custom fields** — Add metadata like "Employee ID", "Office Location", or "Department". See [Custom fields](/help/documentation/custom-fields).
- **Localisation** — Set individual timezone, language, and date format preferences.

---

## Schedules, billing, and costs on people

People are one of the main entities where you configure work patterns and financial tracking:

- **Work schedules** — Define this person's standard working hours and days. Schedules can also be inherited from tags or set at the organisation level. See [Work schedules](/help/documentation/work-schedule).
- **Billing rates** — Set how much this person's time is billed. See [Billing rates](/help/documentation/billing).
- **Cost rates** — Track this person's internal cost. See [Cost rates](/help/documentation/costs).
- **Absence quotas** — Define individual time-off allowances. See [Time off](/help/documentation/timeoff).

<Info>
  Rates and schedules follow a priority system: settings on a person override those inherited from tags or the organisation. See [Billing rates](/help/documentation/billing) for the full priority order.
</Info>

## Archiving and offboarding

<Warning>
  Do not delete a person if you want to keep their historical time data. Use **Archive** instead — this frees up a seat while preserving all their records for reports.
</Warning>

To archive a person, open their profile and click **Archive**. You can unarchive them at any time.

For bulk operations, select multiple people from the list and use the bulk **Archive** or **Unarchive** actions.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when someone leaves the company?">
    Archive their profile instead of deleting it. This preserves all their historical time data for your reports while freeing up the user seat on your subscription.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I create custom roles beyond Admin, Manager, and Employee?">
    Yes. Go to **Settings** > **Roles** to create custom roles with granular permissions. See [Roles & permissions](/help/documentation/roles-authorisations).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I add multiple people at once?">
    Yes. Use the **Import** option in the People module to paste names and emails from a spreadsheet.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I sign in as another user for troubleshooting?">
    Yes. Administrators can use the **Admin takeover** feature to sign in as another person. This is useful for diagnosing issues or verifying what a team member sees.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
