Beebole's account settings let you configure how your organisation works within the platform. From enabling specific modules to setting regional preferences, these settings shape the experience for everyone in your account.

<Info>
  Only administrators can access and modify account settings.
</Info>

---

## Accessing account settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the settings page">
    In the left sidebar, click **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Account">
    Click the **Account** tab to view your organisation's general settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Feature toggles

Beebole is modular by design. You can enable or disable entire features depending on what your organisation needs. This keeps the interface clean and focused for your team.

The following modules can be toggled on or off:

| Module | What it controls |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Projects** | Project-based time tracking and project management |
| **Tasks** | Task-level tracking within projects |
| **Schedules** | Work schedules and expected hours |
| **Absence types** | Time-off categories and leave management |
| **Expenses** | Expense tracking alongside time entries |
| **Custom fields** | Additional data fields on entities |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle features on or off">
    Use the switches next to each module name to enable or disable it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save**. The changes take effect immediately for all users.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Disabling a module hides it from the interface but does not delete any existing data. You can re-enable it at any time to restore access.
</Tip>

## Localisation

These settings control the default regional preferences for your entire organisation. Individual users may override some of these in their personal profile.

### Timezone and country

Set your organisation's **Timezone** and **Country**. The timezone determines the default for all date and time calculations in Beebole.

### Currency

Choose the **Currency** used across your account for billing rates, costs, budgets, and expenses.

### Language

Select the default **Language** for your organisation. Beebole supports multiple languages, and individual users can change their own language preference.

### Date and time format

Configure how dates appear throughout the interface:

- **Date format** -- Choose between formats like DD/MM/YYYY, MM/DD/YYYY, or YYYY-MM-DD.
- **First day of week** -- Set whether the week starts on **Monday**, **Sunday**, or another day. This affects timesheets, calendars, and reports.

---

## Duration format

Beebole offers three ways to display time durations across the platform:

| Format | Example | Best for |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **hh:mm** | 7:30 | Teams that think in hours and minutes |
| **Decimal** | 7.50 | Billing and invoicing workflows |
| **Hour-string-minute** | 7h 30m | Quick visual readability |

To change the duration format, select your preferred option under **Duration format** in the account settings.

---

## Number formatting

Regional number formatting ensures that values display correctly for your team:

- **Decimal separator** -- Choose between a period (.) or comma (,).
- **Thousand separator** -- Choose between a comma (,), period (.), space, or none.

These settings affect how numbers appear in timesheets, reports, costs, and budgets.

---

## Time format

Choose between **12-hour** (AM/PM) and **24-hour** time display. This setting applies to all time pickers and time displays across your Beebole account.

## Deleting your organisation

You can permanently delete your Beebole organisation and all its data. This is an irreversible action.

<Warning>
  Deleting your organisation removes everything -- all people, projects, time entries, reports, and configuration. This cannot be undone. Export any data you need before proceeding.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to the danger zone">
    At the bottom of the page, find the **Delete organisation** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Click **Delete organisation** and follow the confirmation prompts. You may be asked to type the organisation name to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do feature toggles delete existing data when I disable a module?">
    No. Disabling a module in Beebole hides it from the interface but preserves all existing data. If you re-enable the module later, your data will still be there.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can individual users override the organisation's localisation settings?">
    Users can override some settings, like language and timezone, in their personal profile. However, organisation-wide settings like currency and duration format apply to everyone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to active subscriptions if I delete my organisation?">
    Deleting your organisation cancels your subscription. Any remaining billing period is forfeited. Make sure to export your data and review your [subscription](/help/documentation/subscription) before proceeding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the currency after I have started tracking costs?">
    Yes, you can change the currency at any time. Beebole does not convert existing values -- it simply changes the currency symbol displayed. Review your financial data after switching currencies.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I set the first day of the week?">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account** and look for the **First day of week** option under date and time format settings. This affects how timesheets and calendars are displayed for everyone in your organisation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
