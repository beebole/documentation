Beebole's account settings let you configure how your organisation works within the platform. From enabling specific modules to setting regional preferences, these settings shape the experience for everyone in your account.

<Info>
  Only administrators can access and modify account settings.
</Info>

---

## Accessing account settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the settings page">
    In the left sidebar, click **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Account">
    Click the **Account** tab to view your organisation's general settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Feature toggles <Badge>Admin only</Badge>

Beebole is modular by design. You can enable or disable entire features depending on what your organisation needs. This keeps the interface clean and focused for your team.

The following modules can be toggled on or off:

| Module | What it controls |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Projects** | Project-based time tracking and project management |
| **Tasks** | Task-level tracking within projects |
| **Schedules** | Work schedules and expected hours |
| **Absence types** | Time-off categories and leave management |
| **Expenses** | Expense tracking alongside time entries |
| **Custom fields** | Additional data fields on entities |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle features on or off">
    Use the switches next to each module name to enable or disable it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save**. The changes take effect immediately for all users.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Disabling a module hides it from the interface but does not delete any existing data. You can re-enable it at any time to restore access.
</Tip>

## Localisation <Badge>Admin only</Badge>

These settings control the default regional preferences for your entire organisation. Individual users may override some of these in their personal profile.

### Timezone and country

Set your organisation's **Timezone** and **Country**. The timezone determines the default for all date and time calculations in Beebole.

### Currency

Choose the **Currency** used across your account for billing rates, costs, budgets, and expenses.

### Language

Select the default **Language** for your organisation. Beebole supports multiple languages, and individual users can change their own language preference.

### Date and time format

Configure how dates appear throughout the interface:

- **Date format** -- Choose between formats like DD/MM/YYYY, MM/DD/YYYY, or YYYY-MM-DD.
- **First day of week** -- Set whether the week starts on **Monday**, **Sunday**, or another day. This affects timesheets, calendars, and reports.

## Organisation logo <Badge>Admin only</Badge>

You can upload a logo for your organisation. The logo appears in the sidebar and in emails sent by Beebole, giving your workspace a branded look for your team.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a logo">
    In the organisation header at the top of the settings page, click the logo area. You can **paste, drop, or click to add the logo of your organisation**. The logo is cropped to a 16:9 rectangle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The logo saves automatically after upload. To remove it, click the logo and choose the clear option.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use a square or wide logo for best results. Beebole crops logos to a 16:9 rectangle, so avoid placing important content at the very edges of the image.
</Tip>

## Organisation accent colour <Badge>Admin only</Badge>

Administrators can set a custom accent colour for the organisation. This colour becomes the interface accent for all members — it affects buttons, highlights, and other branded elements throughout Beebole.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the colour picker">
    In the organisation header, click the colour swatch next to the logo. Select the colour you want from the palette.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply">
    The colour updates immediately for all users in the organisation. To reset to the default, open the colour picker again and choose **Remove color**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The accent colour is an organisation-wide setting and applies to all members. Individual users can also pick their own personal interface colour from their user profile, which overrides the organisation default for them alone.
</Note>

## Personal appearance settings

Each user can adjust their own interface independently from the organisation's defaults. These settings apply only to the individual account and do not affect other members.

### Personal interface colour

In addition to the organisation accent colour, each user can choose their own personal colour. Your personal colour overrides the organisation default for you alone.

To set your personal colour, click your profile avatar in the top bar and select **Pick your color**. Choose a colour from the palette. To revert to the organisation colour, open the picker again and select **Remove color**.

### Theme

Beebole supports three visual themes:

| Theme | Behaviour |
|-------|-----------|
| **Light** | Standard light background |
| **Dark** | Dark background, easier on the eyes in low-light environments |
| **Auto** | Automatically follows your device's system theme |

To switch themes, click your profile avatar and select your preferred **Dark / Lite** option. The change takes effect immediately and is saved to your account.

<Note>
  Theme is a personal setting and is not visible to other users. See also [Mobile & Desktop App](/help/documentation/mobile) for more about theme options on mobile.
</Note>

### Language

Beebole's interface is available in multiple languages. Each user can set their own preferred language independently of the organisation default.

To change your language, go to **Settings** > **Account** and locate the **Your language** setting, or ask your administrator to update it on your person profile. The interface updates immediately after saving.

The organisation administrator sets the default **Language** for all new users. Individual users can override it for their own session.

### Profile picture

Each user can upload a profile picture (avatar). Your picture appears next to your name across timesheets, reports, and approval workflows.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile">
    Click your profile avatar in the top bar and select **Your profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a picture">
    Click the avatar area and use the image picker. You can **paste, drop, or click to add an image**. The crop tool lets you adjust framing and zoom before saving. The final image is cropped to a circle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Confirm the crop and save. Your picture updates immediately across the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use a square photo for best results. The crop tool lets you zoom and pan before committing, so you can frame your face precisely.
</Tip>

## Duration format

Beebole offers three ways to display time durations across the platform:

| Format | Example | Best for |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **hh:mm** | 7:30 | Teams that think in hours and minutes |
| **Decimal** | 7.50 | Billing and invoicing workflows |
| **Hour-string-minute** | 7h 30m | Quick visual readability |

To change the duration format, select your preferred option under **Duration format** in the account settings.

---

## Number formatting

Regional number formatting ensures that values display correctly for your team:

- **Decimal separator** -- Choose between a period (.) or comma (,).
- **Thousand separator** -- Choose between a comma (,), period (.), space, or none.

These settings affect how numbers appear in timesheets, reports, costs, and budgets.

---

## Time format

Choose between **12-hour** (AM/PM) and **24-hour** time display. This setting applies to all time pickers and time displays across your Beebole account.

## Deleting your organisation <Badge>Admin only</Badge>

You can permanently delete your Beebole organisation and all its data. This is an irreversible action.

<Warning>
  Deleting your organisation removes everything -- all people, projects, time entries, reports, and configuration. This cannot be undone. Export any data you need before proceeding.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to the danger zone">
    At the bottom of the page, find the **Delete organisation** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Click **Delete organisation** and follow the confirmation prompts. You may be asked to type the organisation name to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do feature toggles delete existing data when I disable a module?">
    No. Disabling a module in Beebole hides it from the interface but preserves all existing data. If you re-enable the module later, your data will still be there.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can individual users override the organisation's localisation settings?">
    Users can override some settings, like language and timezone, in their personal profile. However, organisation-wide settings like currency and duration format apply to everyone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to active subscriptions if I delete my organisation?">
    Deleting your organisation cancels your subscription. Any remaining billing period is forfeited. Make sure to export your data and review your [subscription](/help/documentation/subscription) before proceeding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the currency after I have started tracking costs?">
    Yes, you can change the currency at any time. Beebole does not convert existing values -- it simply changes the currency symbol displayed. Review your financial data after switching currencies.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I set the first day of the week?">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account** and look for the **First day of week** option under date and time format settings. This affects how timesheets and calendars are displayed for everyone in your organisation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can individual users choose a different interface colour from the organisation colour?">
    Yes. Each user can set their own personal accent colour by clicking their profile avatar and selecting **Pick your color**. The personal colour overrides the organisation colour for that user only. To revert, open the colour picker and choose **Remove color**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I change the interface language for my own account?">
    Go to **Settings** > **Account** and change the **Your language** setting. This updates the interface language for your account only and does not affect other users.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
