Beebole keeps your team informed through in-app and email notifications. Notifications alert you to approval actions, mentions, timesheet reminders, and other events — so nothing slips through the cracks.

---

## Notification types

Beebole sends notifications for the following events:

| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Timesheet submitted** | A team member submits their timesheet for approval |
| **Approval action** | A timesheet is approved, rejected, or sent back for changes |
| **Mentions** | Someone @mentions you in a journal message |
| **Timesheet reminders** | Automated reminders for unfilled or unsubmitted timesheets |
| **Approval reminders** | Automated reminders for pending approval actions |
| **Team events** | New team members, schedule changes, and other organizational updates |

---

## Notification channels

### In-app notifications

Notifications appear in the **Journal** with unread indicators. Beebole shows a badge count for unread notifications, and uses a "new" separator to help you quickly find what you haven't seen yet.

### Email notifications

Beebole sends email notifications for important events. Emails include relevant context — for example, approval notification emails include a summary of the submitted timesheet.

<Info>
  You can approve or reject timesheets directly from the email notification without opening Beebole.
</Info>

### Digest batching

Instead of receiving individual emails for every event, Beebole can aggregate notifications into periodic digests. This reduces email volume while keeping you informed.

## Configuring your notification preferences

Each person can customize which notifications they receive:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile settings">
    Click your profile picture or name, then select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Notifications">
    Select the **Notifications** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your preferences">
    Toggle notifications on or off for each event type. Choose between individual emails and digest batching.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Every notification email includes a one-click **Unsubscribe** link, making it easy to manage your preferences directly from your inbox.
</Tip>

## Timesheet reminders

Beebole can automatically remind team members to fill in and submit their timesheets. Administrators configure reminders at the organisation level:

- **Reminder frequency** — Choose when reminders are sent (e.g., daily, weekly).
- **Trigger conditions** — Reminders fire when a timesheet is incomplete or unsubmitted past a configurable number of days.
- **Scope** — Apply reminders to everyone, or configure them per person.

---

## Approval reminders

Separate from timesheet reminders, approval reminders notify approvers when timesheets are waiting for their action. This helps prevent bottlenecks in the [approval workflow](/documentation/approval).

---

## Email templates

Administrators can customize the content of notification emails:

- Edit the subject line and body text for different notification types.
- Include dynamic fields (person name, project name, date range) that populate automatically.
- Preview templates before activating them.

To manage email templates, go to **Settings** > **Email templates**.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I turn off all email notifications?">
    Yes. In your notification preferences, disable email for each event type. You will still see notifications in the in-app Journal.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can an administrator configure notification settings for the whole team?">
    Administrators control organisation-wide settings like reminder frequency and email templates. Individual notification preferences (which events to receive) are managed by each person.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if an email notification fails to deliver?">
    Beebole automatically retries failed notification deliveries. If the issue persists, the notification remains available in the in-app Journal.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I respond to approval notifications by email?">
    Yes. You can approve or reject timesheets directly from the notification email. You can also reply to journal messages via email.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
