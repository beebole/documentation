## Subscription

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a seat?">
    A seat represents one active person in your Beebole account. Your subscription is based on the number of seats you use — you pay per active person. When you add someone, an additional seat is billed. When you archive someone, the seat is released at the next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does Beebole cost?">
    Beebole offers seat-based pricing with monthly and annual billing options. Annual billing provides a discount compared to monthly payments. Visit the [Subscription & Billing](/help/documentation/subscription) page for details on pricing plans and how to manage your subscription.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I try Beebole before subscribing?">
    Yes. Beebole offers a free trial with full access to all features. You can add as many projects and people as you need to properly evaluate the tool during the trial period. Contact support@beebole.com if you have any questions about trial limits.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
