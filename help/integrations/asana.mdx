## Why integrate Beebole with Asana
Asana is an excellent Project Management tool. If you are already using Asana to assign and complete your team activities, you can integrate with Beebole to simplify time tracking on those same activities.

## What's included
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Synchonized projects, tasks and sub-tasks">
    Synchonize all your projects and tasks in Beebole when you first connect and update new projects and tasks 
    automatically when you create or modify them in Asana.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Employees automatically imported">
    Import all your existing employees when you first synchronize your account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step by step configuration
<Steps>
    <Step title="Review Categories">Review the existing categories and update or

    <Step title="Connect to Asana">Click on the button to connect to your Asana account and start the sync process (_It may take a few min depending on your Asana account)_.<br/><br/>
