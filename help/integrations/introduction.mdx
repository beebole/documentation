Beebole integrates with the tools your team already uses, so you can track time without switching between applications. Import projects and tasks from your project management tools, sync calendar events to your timesheet, sign in with your existing accounts, and export time data to your accounting software.

All integrations are configured from **Settings** > **Integrations** in your Beebole account. Each integration can be enabled, disabled, and reconfigured independently.

<Info>Most integrations are available on Pro and Enterprise plans. You need administrative privileges in Beebole to set up integrations.</Info>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I find integrations in Beebole?">
    All integrations are managed from **Settings** > **Integrations** in your Beebole account. You need administrative privileges to access this page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use multiple integrations at the same time?">
    Yes. You can enable multiple integrations simultaneously. For example, you can import projects from Asana, sync your Google Calendar, and export data to QuickBooks all at once.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I disable an integration?">
    Disabling an integration stops future data syncing, but all previously imported data (projects, tasks, people) remains in Beebole as local records. You can re-enable the integration at any time to resume syncing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do integrations require a specific Beebole plan?">
    Most integrations are available on Pro and Enterprise plans. Calendar integrations and SSO are available to all users. Check each integration page for specific plan requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I build my own integration with Beebole?">
    Yes. Beebole provides a [GraphQL API](/help/integrations/custom-integrations) that you can use to build custom integrations with any tool or internal system.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
